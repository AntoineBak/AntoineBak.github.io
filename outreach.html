I took part in the RJMI of INRIA Paris that took place in October 2024 by animating two 2-hours workshops with a group of 6 high school students. This workshop gives a high school-level introduction to a research area in mathematics through problem solving.

This event is aimed at high school girls who are interested in mathematics and computer science. Through workshops and informal discussions with mathematicians and computer scientists, we encourage them to pursue a scientific career and give them a better knowledge of the possible path in these fields.
